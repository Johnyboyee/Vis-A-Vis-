About Our Place

Come experience the charm and warmth of Vis a Vis Lounge, located in the heart of 4th Flr Maharlika Livelihood Center, Baguio City. Step into a cozy haven where comfort meets elegance, perfect for unwinding after a long day or catching up with friends. Our lounge offers a delightful range of handcrafted cocktails and exquisite dishes, each prepared with care using fresh, local ingredients. Whether you prefer a quiet corner indoors or the fresh air of our outdoor patio, you'll find a spot to relax and enjoy the ambiance.

Live music sets the mood on select evenings, adding to the inviting atmosphere. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply seeking a memorable night out, Vis a Vis Lounge promises a delightful experience that keeps guests coming back.
